## Lecture 1: Introduction, Course Overview
  * [Google Slides](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1vLxKNR0kUxHkpt9Pxs0xfbxgMvZ_KnxkbA4KfiQjfVQ/edit?usp=sharing)
