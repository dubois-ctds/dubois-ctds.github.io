title: "About the Curriculum"
---

This introductory data science course curriculum for undergraduates is split into modules. Computational thinking course instructors that are looking for data science applications are especially encouraged to adapt this work in all or in part. Please contact the PIs/Team Members if you have questions.

Where applicable, materials that are classroom tested will be noted with an asterisk (\*).
